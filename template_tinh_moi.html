TINH MOI TRONG NCKH

Novelty / Originality - Dac diem quan trong nhat cua Nghien cuu Khoa hoc

1. Tinh Moi La Gi? DEFINITION

Dinh Nghia

Tinh moi (Novelty/Originality) la dac diem hang dau cua NCKH. Mot cong trinh NCKH phai tao ra tri thuc moi - nhung dieu ma truoc do chua ai biet, chua ai lam, hoac chua ai chung minh duoc.

Theo slide bai giang: NCKH la qua trinh thu thap thong tin co he thong nham phat minh ra nhung cai moi ve ban chat su vat, ve the gioi TN & XH.

Tai Sao Tinh Moi Quan Trong?

1. La tieu chi so 1 de danh gia chat luong NCKH

2. Quyet dinh bai bao co duoc chap nhan dang hay khong

3. La co so de cap patent, bao ho so huu tri tue

4. Dong gop vao kho tang tri thuc nhan loai

5. Tao gia tri ung dung thuc tien cho xa hoi

2. 5 Cap Do Tinh Moi (Tu Thap Den Cao) LEVELS

1
Cai Tien (Improvement) DE NHAT
Cai tien, nang cap PP/bai toan/he thong hien co. Khac phuc nhuoc diem cu, giu lai uu diem. Theo slide: "Van de hien huu con nhuoc diem => cai tien (ke thua hoac moi)"
VI DU

"De xuat thuat toan X cai tien, giam 30% thoi gian tinh toan so voi thuat toan X goc"

2
Tich Hop (Integration/Combination) TRUNG BINH
Ket hop 2+ PP/ly thuyet cu co uu-nhuoc diem doi nghich de tao PP moi tot hon. Theo slide: "Hai PP hien huu co nhuoc diem va uu diem doi nghich => Tich hop cho ra PP chung"
VI DU

"Ket hop PP DLV (nhanh nhung khong chinh xac) va PP toi uu hoa tong the (chinh xac nhung cham) => PP hon hop vua nhanh vua chinh xac" (VD tu slide)

3
Goc Nhin Moi (New Perspective) KHO
Du lieu cu + goc nhin moi, hoac bai toan cu + PP giai moi hoan toan. Ap dung ly thuyet tu linh vuc khac vao linh vuc dang NC. Theo slide: "Du lieu cu => Goc nhin moi; Bai toan cu => PP giai moi"
VI DU

"Ap dung mo hinh deep learning (tu Computer Science) de phan tich du lieu y te truyen thong"

4
Van De Moi (New Problem) CHUYEN GIA
Phat hien va giai quyet van de/bai toan moi hoan toan ma chua ai dat ra truoc do. Theo slide: "Van de/bai toan moi hoan toan => Y tuong cu hoac moi de giai quyet"
VI DU

"Phat hien hien tuong moi trong truyen thong khong day 6G va de xuat mo hinh ly thuyet giai thich"

5
Phat Minh / Sang Che (Invention) CAP CAO NHAT
Tao ra ly thuyet moi, nguyen ly moi, hoac giai phap ky thuat hoan toan moi co the ap dung vao thuc tien. Tuong ung voi san pham "Phat minh" va "Sang che" trong NCKH.
VI DU

"Tim ra vaccine COVID-19 dua tren cong nghe mRNA - nguyen ly hoan toan moi trong san xuat vaccine"

3. Quy Trinh The Hien Tinh Moi Trong Bai NC WORKFLOW

1

Doc Tai Lieu

Tong quan 20-50 bai bao lien quan. Tim research gap.

→
2

Xac Dinh Gap

Tim nhuoc diem, han che, khoang trong cua NC truoc.

→
3

De Xuat Moi

Trinh bay y tuong/PP/mo hinh moi cua ban.

→
4

Chung Minh

Thuc nghiem/ly thuyet chung minh PP moi tot hon.

→
5

So Sanh

So sanh ket qua voi PP cu, lam noi bat dong gop moi.

4. Template Viet - Nhan Manh Tinh Moi TEMPLATE

1
Dat Van De (Neu Research Gap)
"Hien nay, cac nghien cuu ve [linh vuc] da dat duoc nhieu ket qua quan trong, tuy nhien van con ton tai [nhuoc diem/han che cu the]. Cu the, [PP/cach tiep can hien tai] con gap van de [van de cu the]. Do do, viec [de xuat giai phap moi] la het suc can thiet."
Huong dan: Chi ro cai gi da lam, cai gi chua lam, tai sao NC cua ban can thiet
2
Trinh Bay Diem Moi (Contribution)
"Dong gop chinh cua nghien cuu nay bao gom:

(1) De xuat [PP/mo hinh/thuat toan moi] dua tren [co so ly thuyet].
(2) Chung minh bang [ly thuyet/thuc nghiem] rang PP de xuat [uu diem cu the: nhanh hon X%, chinh xac hon Y%] so voi cac PP hien co.
(3) Ung dung thanh cong vao [bai toan/tinh huong thuc te]."
Huong dan: Liet ke 2-4 dong gop moi, moi dong gop phai cu the va do luong duoc
3
So Sanh Voi NC Truoc (Novelty Comparison)
"Khac voi nghien cuu cua [Tac gia, Nam] su dung [PP cu], nghien cuu nay de xuat [PP moi] voi cac diem khac biet chinh:

- [Diem khac 1]: ...
- [Diem khac 2]: ...
- [Diem khac 3]: ...

Ket qua cho thay PP de xuat dat [chi so cu the], vuot troi hon [X%] so voi [PP cu]."
Huong dan: Dung bang so sanh (table) de the hien ro su khac biet va uu viet
4
Gia Thuyet Nghien Cuu (Lien Ket Voi Tinh Moi)
"H1: PP [ten PP moi] de xuat co hieu suat [cao hon / tot hon] so voi PP [ten PP cu] trong viec [nhiem vu cu the].

H2: Viec ket hop [yeu to A] va [yeu to B] se tao ra ket qua [tot hon / moi] so voi viec su dung rieng le."
Huong dan: Gia thuyet phai the hien ro: cai moi cua ban (bien doc lap) => ket qua tot hon (bien phu thuoc)
5
Ket Luan (Tong Ket Dong Gop Moi)
"Nghien cuu nay da dong gop vao linh vuc [ten linh vuc] voi cac ket qua moi sau:

Ve ly thuyet: De xuat [mo hinh/ly thuyet moi] giai thich [hien tuong].
Ve thuc tien: Cung cap [giai phap/cong cu] giup [doi tuong] giai quyet [van de] hieu qua hon [X%].
Han che va huong phat trien: NC con han che o [...]. Huong NC tiep theo co the [mo rong/cai tien them]."
Huong dan: Phan biet ro dong gop ly thuyet vs dong gop thuc tien

5. Mau Cau Thuong Dung De The Hien Tinh Moi PHRASES

English
"To the best of our knowledge, this is the first study that..." / "Unlike previous studies, our approach..." / "The novelty of this work lies in..." / "The main contribution of this paper is..."
Tieng Viet
"Theo hieu biet cua chung toi, day la nghien cuu dau tien..." / "Khac voi cac NC truoc, nghien cuu nay de xuat..." / "Diem moi cua nghien cuu nam o..." / "Dong gop chinh cua bai bao la..."
Gap
"However, no previous study has addressed..." / "There is a lack of research on..." / "The existing methods suffer from..." / "Despite recent advances, challenges remain in..."
Gap VN
"Tuy nhien, chua co NC nao giai quyet..." / "Con thieu cac NC ve..." / "Cac PP hien tai con han che o..." / "Mac du da co nhieu tien bo, van con thach thuc trong..."

6. Nen Va Khong Nen DO & DON'T

NEN LAM

  • Chi ro research gap cu the tu tong quan tai lieu
  • Liet ke dong gop moi dang numbered list (1, 2, 3...)
  • So sanh dinh luong voi PP cu (bang so lieu, %)
  • Dung bang so sanh (comparison table) de the hien su khac biet
  • Trinh bay tinh moi ngay tu Abstract va Introduction
  • Lien ket tinh moi voi muc tieu va gia thuyet NC
  • Dung cau "To the best of our knowledge, this is the first..."
  • Neu ro dong gop ly thuyet VA dong gop thuc tien

KHONG NEN LAM

  • Chi noi "chua co ai lam" ma khong chung minh bang tong quan TL
  • Noi mo ho: "NC nay co dong gop moi" ma khong cu the hoa
  • Lap lai nhung gi NC truoc da lam roi tuyen bo la "moi"
  • Qua pho truong tinh moi ma khong co bang chung
  • Bo qua viec trich dan va so sanh voi NC truoc
  • Chi so sanh dinh tinh ma khong co so lieu cu the
  • Nham lan "moi voi toi" va "moi voi the gioi" (da co nguoi lam)
  • De tinh moi chi o phan ket luan ma khong nhan manh tu dau

7. Checklist Kiem Tra Tinh Moi SELF-CHECK

1 Truoc Khi Viet

  • Da doc du tong quan tai lieu (20-50 bai)?
  • Da xac dinh ro research gap?
  • Y tuong chua ai thuc hien truoc (kiem tra Google Scholar)?
  • Co the mo ta dong gop moi trong 1-2 cau?

2 Trong Khi Viet

  • Abstract co nhan manh tinh moi?
  • Introduction co research gap ro rang?
  • Co phan "Contributions" liet ke dong gop moi?
  • Co bang so sanh voi cac PP truoc?

3 Sau Khi Viet

  • Ket qua chung minh PP moi tot hon PP cu?
  • So sanh co so lieu cu the (%, thoi gian, do chinh xac)?
  • Ket luan tom tat dong gop moi ro rang?
  • Reviewer co the nhan ra tinh moi trong 2 phut doc?
