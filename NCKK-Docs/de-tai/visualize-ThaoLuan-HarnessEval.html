4. 10 Cau hoi kho nhat Hoi dong se hoi

1 "Ban noi do harness 'doc lap voi LLM.' Nhung tool dispatch phu thuoc LLM quyet dinh goi tool nao. Lam sao tach roi?"
Hoi vi: Day la gia dinh nen tang cua de tai — neu khong tra loi duoc, toan bo framework vo nghia.
Goi y tra loi:
Harness quyet dinh CUNG CAP tools nao, format description ra sao, retry policy. LLM quyet dinh CHON tool. Giong nhu danh gia nha hang qua thuc don va dich vu, khong phai khau vi khach hang. Dung ANOVA 2 chieu (harness x LLM) de phan tach variance.
2 "Tai sao Princeton (SWE-Agent) va Anthropic (Claude Code) khong tu lam? Co phai vi ho biet rang khong can thiet?"
Hoi vi: Neu nguoi tao ra harness khong thay can danh gia, gap co the la "artificial".
Goi y tra loi:
(1) Ho la developers, khong phai evaluators — ho co incentive de xuat harness moi, khong phai so sanh. (2) Linh vuc tang truong qua nhanh (2023-2026), chua ai dung lai meta-evaluate. (3) Lou et al. (2026) da gian tiep acknowledge khi so sanh harness tren game tasks.
3 "16,000 evaluations nghe nhieu, nhung moi condition chi 200 data points. Power analysis cho thay can bao nhieu samples?"
Hoi vi: Voi 11 metrics va Bonferroni correction, 200 co the khong du.
Goi y tra loi:
Two-sample t-test, d=0.5, alpha=0.05, power=0.80 → can ~64 samples/group. 200 du cho individual comparison. Nhung voi multiple comparison correction (80 conditions) → alpha giam → can trinh bay power analysis chinh thuc.
4 "M1.1 dung 'ground-truth tool sequence tu expert.' Nhung 1 task co 5 cach giai dung voi 5 tool sequences. Ground-truth la gi?"
Hoi vi: Construct validity — metric co do dung thu can do khong?
Goi y tra loi:
Ground-truth nen la TAP HOP cac sequences chap nhan duoc (set of acceptable sequences). Dinh nghia equivalence classes (vd: grep+read = search+read ve muc dich). Dung 2-3 annotators, do inter-annotator agreement.
5 "Modular harness tu xay trong 1 thang — SWE-Agent phat trien 2 nam voi doi Princeton. Khi so sanh va modular harness thua, ban ket luan gi?"
Hoi vi: Unfair comparison + conflict of interest (tu xay, tu danh gia).
Goi y tra loi:
Muc dich khong phai so sanh "harness nao tot hon" ma do DONG GOP tung component. Modular harness la cong cu thuc nghiem (tat/bat), khong phai san pham. Tuy nhien nen FORK SWE-Agent lam base de dam bao quality.
6 "Chi test tren Python (SWE-bench). Harness tot cho Python co tot cho Java, Rust khong?"
Hoi vi: External validity — ket qua generalize duoc toi dau?
Goi y tra loi:
Acknowledge la limitation. SWE-bench Verified la benchmark pho bien nhat, dung lam starting point. Tuong lai mo rong voi SWE-Compass (10 ngon ngu). Tool system cho Python (pytest, pip) khac Java (maven, junit) → can caution khi generalize.
7 "Tool system dong gop 35%. So nay co y nghia gi cho developer? Ho nen lam gi?"
Hoi vi: Actionability — nghien cuu co gia tri thuc tien khong?
Goi y tra loi:
Can di sau hon: tool CU THE nao quan trong nhat? grep? edit? bash? Config nao toi uu? Them muc "Design Guidelines" derive tu ket qua — vd: "Toi thieu can 5 core tools. Them tools chi tang 2-3%."
8 "LLM outputs khong deterministic. Ban chay 1 run/condition. Lam sao dam bao reproducible?"
Hoi vi: Reproducibility la nen tang cua khoa hoc.
Goi y tra loi:
(1) Set temperature=0, (2) chay 3 runs cho conditions quan trong nhat, report mean +/- std, (3) report confidence intervals. Trade-off: 3 runs x 80 conditions = 48,000 evaluations → gap 3x budget. Giai phap: 3 runs cho 15 conditions chinh, 1 run cho phan con lai.
9 "Ban dung GPT-judge de do M4.2 (Instruction Adherence). LLM danh gia LLM — co circular reasoning khong?"
Hoi vi: Validity of using LLM to judge LLM.
Goi y tra loi:
Validate GPT-judge voi human judgment tren sample (tinh correlation). Dung GPT KHAC model voi model duoc danh gia (Claude judge GPT agent va nguoc lai). Report inter-judge agreement.
10 "Tai sao 5 chieu ma khong phai 3? Ban co the chi dung 'resolve rate khi doi backend' (1 metric) de chung minh harness quan trong."
Hoi vi: Ocam's razor — giai phap don gian nhat co du khong?
Goi y tra loi:
Portability chi la 1 goc nhin. Developer can biet TAI SAO harness A tot hon B — khong chi biet RANG A tot hon B. 5 chieu cho diagnostic power. Tuy nhien, chien luoc tot hon: BAT DAU voi 2-3 chieu, mo rong sau.

5. 7 Goi y cai thien (theo muc do uu tien)

1 Thu hep scope — 3 chieu thay vi 5
Giu 3 chieu cot loi: D1 (Tool Dispatch), D2 (Context), D5 (Portability). D3 (Safety) va D4 (Session) de cho future work.
→ 3 chieu x 3 harnesses x 3 backends = 27 conditions (thay vi 80) → kha thi 6 thang
→ Giam chi phi tu $3,000-6,000 xuong $1,000-2,000
2 Fork SWE-Agent, khong viet harness tu dau
SWE-Agent la open-source (MIT). Fork va refactor thanh modular: doi tool set, doi backend, doi context strategy chi can modify 1 module. Giam GD2 tu 2-3 thang xuong 2-3 tuan.
3 Them pilot study (5 conditions x 20 tasks)
Chen 2 tuan giua GD1 va GD2. Muc dich: validate metrics phan biet duoc khong, uoc tinh cost chinh xac, debug pipeline. Neu 2 conditions khong khac biet → merge → giam tong conditions.
4 Them power analysis + threats to validity
Trinh bay: sample size, effect size, alpha, power. Them 3 loai validity: internal, external, construct. Them multiple comparison correction (Bonferroni/FDR).
5 Bo phan "Du kien ket qua" hoac viet trung tinh
"Tool dong gop 30-40%" tao confirmation bias. Thay bang hypotheses: "H1: Tool system co effect size lon nhat." De ket qua thuc nghiem tu noi.
6 Dinh nghia formal "independence" cua harness evaluation
Dung ANOVA 2 chieu (harness x LLM) de phan tach variance. Chay CUNG harness voi NHIEU LLM → phan nao do harness, phan nao do LLM. Day la gia tri khoa hoc lon nhat cua de tai.
7 Them papers tu SE venues (ICSE, FSE, ASE)
Hien tai hau het la arXiv preprints. Can them peer-reviewed papers. Kiem tra co paper nao da lam "evaluation of agent infrastructure" ngoai coding domain. Xac minh papers 2026 chua peer review.

3 DIEU CAN LAM NGAY
1. Thu hep scope

5 → 3 chieu, 80 → 25-30 conditions. Tinh lai chi phi va timeline. Mot de tai kha thi va hoan thanh co gia tri hon de tai tham vong nhung dang do.

2. Lam ro metrics

Dinh nghia formal cho M1.1 (ground-truth la gi?), M2.2 (token waste la gi?), M4.2 (validate GPT-judge). Moi metric can: definition, operationalization, validation, limitations.

3. Them pilot study

5 conditions x 20 tasks = 100 evaluations. Validate metrics, uoc tinh cost, debug pipeline TRUOC KHI cam ket 16,000 evaluations.
