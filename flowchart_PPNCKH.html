6. Suy Luan Khoa Hoc

Suy Dien (Deduction)

Theo Aristotle: Suy luan di tu cai chung toi cai rieng. Can co tien de chinh + tien de phu = Ket luan.

Chung ➔ Rieng
+

Quy Nap (Induction)

Theo Francis Bacon: Kien thuc dat duoc di tu thong tin rieng den ket luan chung. Dung tien de rieng da duoc chap nhan.

Rieng ➔ Chung
➔

Suy Luan Khoa Hoc

Ket hop Suy dien + Quy nap. Gom: Xac dinh gia thuyet ➔ Phan tich kien thuc ➔ Ket luan gia thuyet.

8. Cac Hinh Thuc To Chuc Nghien Cuu

a

De Tai

Mot nhiem vu NC cu the, do ca nhan/nhom thuc hien. Co muc tieu, noi dung, PP ro rang.

b

Du An KH

De tai mang tinh ung dung, co rang buoc thoi gian & nguon luc. Huong toi hieu qua KT-XH.

c

Chuong Trinh KH

Tap hop cac de tai/du an co cung muc dich. Co tinh dong bo, ho tro lan nhau.

d

De An KH

Van kien trinh cap quan ly/co quan tai tro. De xuat thanh lap to chuc hoac tai tro hoat dong.
